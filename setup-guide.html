Setup guide

Reseller Finance Tracker

Track income, expenses, and your Schedule C in one place. Data syncs across every device you sign into.

Open the app ↗
First time setup
1

Sign in with a magic link

Enter your email on the sign-in screen and hit Send magic link. Check your inbox, click the link, and you are in. No password ever.

Your data is tied to your email and syncs across every device you sign in on.

2

Set up your lists in Settings

Before adding any data go to Settings and build out your custom lists. These populate every dropdown in the app.

  • Income channels — where you sell (eBay, Amazon, Facebook, Poshmark, etc.)
  • Income accounts — where money lands (BOA, Cash, Venmo, PayPal, etc.)
  • Expense accounts — cards and accounts you buy with
  • Expense categories — pre-filled with Schedule C lines, add any custom ones
  • Platform — where inventory is destined (Local is pre-filled, add eBay, Amazon FBA, etc.)

Type a value and hit Add. Hit X to remove. Changes save instantly.

3

Add your first entries

Use the Quick Add Income and Quick Add Expense panels on the Dashboard for fast entries. For full detail use the + Income and - Expense pages in the sidebar.

Net profit calculates automatically: Payout minus COGS minus Shipping.

4

Watch the dashboard update

Revenue, expenses, net profit, gross margin, and estimated quarterly tax all update in real time as you add entries. Charts show revenue by channel and expenses by category.

Auto-pull from eBay and Amazon (optional)
⚡ Auto-pull imports your eBay payouts and Amazon settlement events automatically. Optional — you can enter everything by hand if you prefer.
1

eBay credentials

  • Go to developer.ebay.com/my/keys
  • Create a Production keyset (not Sandbox)
  • Add scope: https://api.ebay.com/oauth/api_scope/sell.finances
  • Generate a User token via OAuth
  • Copy App ID, Cert ID, Dev ID, and User Token into Settings
2

Amazon credentials

  • Go to Seller Central → Apps and Services → Develop Apps
  • Create an app, request Finances and Orders roles
  • Capture Client ID, Client Secret, and Refresh Token
  • US Marketplace ID is pre-filled as ATVPDKIKX0DER
  • Paste all values into Settings → Amazon SP-API Credentials → Save
3

Pull your data

Go to Settings → Auto-Pull. Set your date range and hit Pull eBay or Pull Amazon. The log shows what was added and what was skipped. Re-pulling the same date range is safe — duplicates are blocked automatically.

Exporting data
1

Export to CSV

Use the Export Report button at the bottom of the sidebar. Downloads income.csv and expenses.csv — both open in Excel or Google Sheets.

2

Download blank Excel template

Also available via Export Report — gives you a pre-structured xlsx with income, expenses, monthly P&L, and a Schedule C summary sheet. Paste your CSVs in at year-end and take the net profit number to your tax preparer.

Frequently asked questions
Will my data be there if I close the browser or switch devices?
Yes. Everything saves to your account. Sign in with the same email on any device and your data is there.
Can I use this on my phone?
Yes. Open the link in your phone browser and bookmark it. The layout is responsive.
What if I accidentally delete an entry?
Deletions are permanent — no undo. Export your CSVs regularly as backup.
What does the estimated tax number show?
A rough estimate of your quarterly self-employment tax based on current net profit: net x 92.35% x 15.3% divided by 4. It updates in real time. Use it as a ballpark, not tax advice.
How do I prepare for Schedule C at year-end?
Export your CSVs from the sidebar, paste them into the blank Excel template, and look at the Schedule C summary sheet. It maps your totals to the right lines. Give those numbers to your tax preparer or enter them into your tax software.
My magic link is not working
Check your spam folder first. Links expire after 1 hour — go back and request a new one. Click the link on the same device you requested it from.
My dropdowns are empty
Go to Settings and add your channels, accounts, categories, and platforms. All dropdowns are fully custom — nothing is pre-filled except expense categories and Local under Platform.
The eBay or Amazon pull is not working
Make sure credentials are saved in Settings. For eBay use a Production keyset — Sandbox keys will not work. For Amazon confirm the proxy URL is filled in and test the connection first.
