Getting started
1

Open the app in your browser

The tracker runs entirely in your browser — nothing to install. Just open the link and bookmark it.

bigman7911.github.io/reseller-tracker

Works on desktop and mobile. Chrome or Edge recommended for best experience.

2

Your data saves automatically

Every entry you add is saved to your browser's local storage instantly. If you close the tab and come back, everything will still be there — on that same device and browser.

⚠ Data is saved per browser, per device. If you switch browsers or clear your browser data, you'll lose your entries. Use the Export CSV buttons regularly as a backup.
3

Add your first income entry

Click + Income in the nav. Fill in the fields and hit Add entry. Net profit calculates automatically from Payout − COGS − Shipping.

Add your expenses

Click - Expense in the nav. Every purchase — inventory, software, supplies, shipping — goes here. The Category field maps directly to Schedule C line items at year-end.

Auto-pull from eBay and Amazon (optional)
⚡ Auto-pull automatically imports your eBay payouts and Amazon settlement events so you don't have to enter them manually. It's optional — you can always enter everything by hand.
1

eBay — get your API credentials

  • Go to developer.ebay.com/my/keys
  • Create a Production keyset (not Sandbox)
  • Add scope: https://api.ebay.com/oauth/api_scope/sell.finances
  • Generate a User token via OAuth
  • Copy: App ID, Cert ID, Dev ID, and User Token
  • Paste all four into API Settings → eBay in the app and hit Save
2

Amazon — get your SP-API credentials

  • Go to Seller Central → Apps & Services → Develop Apps
  • Create an app and request Finances and Orders roles
  • Authorize the app and capture: Client ID, Client Secret, Refresh Token
  • US Marketplace ID is ATVPDKIKX0DER (already filled in)
  • Paste into API Settings → Amazon in the app and hit Save
3

Amazon proxy URL

Amazon's API requires a server-side proxy to work from the browser. Your setup includes a Cloudflare Worker that handles this. Ask your account manager for the proxy URL and paste it into the Auto-pull → Cloudflare Worker URL field.

Hit Test connection first to confirm it's reachable before pulling.

4

Pull your data

Go to the Auto-pull tab. Set your date range, then hit Pull eBay payouts or Pull Amazon settlements. The pull log will show you exactly what was added and what was skipped (already exists). You can re-pull the same date range safely — duplicates are automatically blocked.

Exporting and backing up
1

Export to CSV

Go to Export tab. Download income.csv and expenses.csv anytime. These open directly in Excel or Google Sheets.

2

Download the blank Excel template

The Export tab also has a Download blank template button. This gives you a pre-structured .xlsx file — income sheet, expenses sheet, monthly P&L, and a Schedule C summary — that you can paste your CSV exports into at year-end.

Frequently asked questions
Will my data be there if I close the browser?
Yes — data auto-saves to your browser's localStorage every time you add an entry. It will be there when you come back, as long as you use the same browser on the same device and haven't cleared your browser data.
Can I use this on my phone?
Yes. Open the link in your phone's browser and bookmark it. The layout is responsive. Local storage works on mobile too so your data persists.
What if I accidentally delete an entry?
Deletions are permanent — there's no undo. Export your CSVs regularly so you always have a backup. The danger zone clear-all also warns you to export first.
Are my API keys safe?
Keys are stored in your browser's localStorage on your own device and are never sent to any server other than eBay and Amazon directly. Don't use this on a shared or public computer.
What does the Dashboard show?
Total revenue, expenses, net profit, gross margin, and entry counts at the top. Below that: bar charts of revenue by channel (Local, eBay, Amazon, Lysted, StubHub) and expenses by category (COGS, other, software, supplies, shipping). All charts update live as you add entries.
How do I prepare for Schedule C at year-end?
Export your income and expenses CSVs from the Export tab, then paste them into the blank Excel template (also on the Export tab). The template has a P&L sheet that maps totals to the correct Schedule C lines automatically. Take those numbers to your tax preparer or enter them directly into your tax software.
The eBay or Amazon pull isn't working — what do I check?
First confirm your credentials are saved in API Settings (the status should show "Configured"). For Amazon, make sure the proxy URL is entered and the Test connection button returns green. For eBay, make sure you're using a Production keyset — Sandbox keys won't work here.
